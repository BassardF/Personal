Project.

BuddyMap is my current IT project. The core purpose is to create a light, design and practical mobile application conceived to help you meet your friends at your own events. After having created the event and invited your friends, BuddyMap will show you their position on the map, how far they are from the meeting point as well as your own way to it. You will nevermore get surprised by an absentminded fiend.
